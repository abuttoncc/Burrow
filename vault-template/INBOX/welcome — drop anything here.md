# Welcome to your INBOX

This folder has **no rules**. Half-thoughts, pasted articles, screenshots-as-links, meeting scraps — drop them in. Nothing here is judged, typed, or schema-checked. Ever.

Rigor happens exactly once, at the gate: run `/burrow-ingest` and everything compilable flows into `wiki/` as typed, time-stamped, linked knowledge — and everything that needs your judgment lands in the review queue.

Try it now: this very note is a valid first meal. (It will compile to nothing — that's a correct outcome. Not everything deserves promotion.)

---

*Sample claim for your first real run, if you want one:*

> On 2026-06-09 the People's Bank of China announced a 50bp cut to the reserve requirement ratio for major banks, effective 2026-06-15, bringing it from 8.00% to 7.50%. Interbank overnight rates drifted lower in the following sessions.

That single paragraph contains a T4 event, a T1 retirement (8.00% → 7.50% with an event stamp), and a T0 observation — a nice tour of the six-tier discipline.
